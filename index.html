Who We Are?

Welcome to Cadets Point Defence Academy in Prayagraj. We are a renowned institution dedicated to comprehensive defense exam preparation. With a focus on nurturing talent and producing exceptional military leaders, we stand at the forefront of defense training in Prayagraj.

Our Academy is built on excellence, integrity, and dedication. Led by experienced faculty, we provide a conducive learning environment that fosters discipline, determination, and holistic development. We specialize in coaching for NDA, CDS, AFCAT, and SSB Interviews, offering the best guidance and support.

At Cadets Point Defence Academy, we understand the challenges of defense exams and strive to equip our cadets with knowledge, skills, and confidence. With state-of-the-art facilities and rigorous training modules, we ensure personalized attention for each cadet.

Join us at Cadets Point Defence Academy and embark on a transformative journey to a successful defense career. Let us shape the future military leaders of our nation.
